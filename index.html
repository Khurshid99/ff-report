6.4 Win rate benchmarklar (CSO Insights)

  • O'rtacha win rate "prognoz qilingan bitimlar" bo'yicha — ~46.4%
  • Sales enablement bo'lmagan kompaniyalarda — ~42.5%
  • Enablement bilan — ~49.0%

6.5 Distributorlar roli (ICTA)

Distributorlar xizmat ko'rsatgan kimyoviy iste'mol ulushi: ~9.7% (2008) → ~12.3% (2022). Specialty-nisha va long-tail mijozlarda distributorlarning roli yuqoriroq.

6.6 Yangi vs mavjud daromad (Alexander Group)

  • Savdoning ~10% yangi accountlardan
  • Savdoning ~12% yangi mahsulotlardan
  • Jami "yangi manbalar" ~22%

6.7 Mijoz fragmentatsiyasi (ACC)

Specialty bozorlarda ko'p accountlar yiliga <$50,000 savdo hosil qiladi — "long tail".

  • Kichik long-tail: o'n minglab $/yil → distributor/digital
  • O'rta bozor: yuz minglab $ → inside sales + texnik qo'llab-quvvatlash
  • Yirik accountlar: millionlab $ → to'g'ridan-to'g'ri KAM + co-creation

6.8 6sense B2B xaridor (2025)

  • O'rtacha xarid sikli: 10.1 oy
  • Xaridorlar o'rtacha 5.1 ta vendor ni ko'radi
  • Xarid guruhi odatda 10+ odam
  • Birinchi murojaat qilgan vendor ~80% holatda yutadi
